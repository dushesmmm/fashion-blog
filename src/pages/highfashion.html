Twitter Reacts to Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi's Louis Vuitton Campaign

The campaign arrives just in time to coincide with the 2022 FIFA World Cup held in Qatar this year. As the players gear up to play for their countries Argentina and Portugal, respectively, the campaign starring two of the greatest footballer of this generation are seen engaging in a game of chess. One may argue that the game of football takes strategic notes from the game of chess. The campaign is photographed by legendary photographer Annie Leibovitz and portrays two of the sport’s greatest legends facing off over a chessboard improves from a Louis Vuitton Damier attaché case.

Minecraft and Burberry Expand Collaboration Lineup with Exclusive Xbox Design

The limited-edition Minecraft x Burberry Xbox arrives in line with the wider collaborative offering which sees the British fashion house take the gaming universe with Burberry: Freedom to Go Beyond as well as a capsule collection inspired by the immersive adventure. Incorporating its heritage pattern: the Nova check, the brand continues to mold its identity around the digital aesthetic with the exclusive console and matching control pad design. Adding a special touch, the Xbox is cased in a one-off packaging with a Minecraft-inspired pixelated arrangement that adopts the check’s brown tones.

Louis Vuitton Releases Virgil Abloh-Designed Pre-Fall 2022 LV Driver Loafers

These moccasin loafers mark the first-ever LV Drivers designed by the late artistic director. The shoe is constructed using two materials and comes in seven different colorways. The first iteration of the LV Driver comes in a monogrammed pattern in grained calfskin and arrives in green, orange, white, Éclipse, and Ébène colorways where the lace tips and soles of the shoes are in contrasting tones. The second version is constructed out of monogram embossed suede and arrives in navy blue and mocha color variations that boast a simple padded sole. This shoe features a single main monogram flower motif inspired by the French gardens.

By JOYCE LI

Burberry Plants Its Quintessential Check in Locations

Maintaining its connection to the Earth, Burberry (LSE:BRBY.L -0.12%) Landscapes is a sustainable series of grand natural artworks made in partnership with Burberry’s creative community. In its latest iteration, the series tells the tales of adventurer Elsie Burberry — the daughter-in-law of the label’s founder, Thomas Burberry — and path-forging aviator Betty Kirby-Green, who led expeditions from Britain to the Canary Islands and South Africa, respectively. In both locations, Burberry Landscapes has touched down.

By ROSS DWYER
