Palace Shares Its Ice-Cold Holiday 2022 Lookbook

Get a closer look at the borg pullovers, fleecy twin-sets, padded essentials and more ahead of the first week’s release.

It’s the end of the Winter 2022 season for Palace, as it now presents a flurry of garments, accessories and must-haves for the Holiday 2022 period. Captured in a lookbook shot by Clare Shilland, we find Palace Skateboards‘ skate team members Savannah Stacey Keenan, Charlie Birch and Kyle Wilson donning the latest and greatest from everybody’s favorite London-based skateboarding stalwart, and this season is nothing short of cozy through-and-through.

From the borg wool zip-up jacket in a starry night shade of blue, complete with stars and frosted Palace branding on the front, to the complementing thick-pile borg bear ear beanie, everything Palace has touched for Holiday 2022 is perfectly fit for the coldest of days — and even colder fits. Elmer the Elephant-esque color-blocking is applied to the full-fleece twin-set tracksuit, while a brown jumper looks to be made from a mohair-like material before intersecting the softness with a hardcore Olde English Palace logo.

Elsewhere, fluffy, fleecy faux fur takes shape on a padded, bulbous puffer jacket, and a very apt Christmas waffle knit makes an appearance with Palace’s branding featuring alongside pouncing cats and fish carcasses. For something more understated, the imprint presents a muted khaki green technical nylon puffer long coat and a black canvas work chore jacket — pieces that can be expertly layered with Palace’s array of T-shirts and long sleeves that are set to arrive too.

From ones that see the moniker adorn a spider web to a long sleeve that plays on Palasonic branding with a VHS stamp reading “Very Heavy Shit,” or even a quilted, padded long sleeve in copper that explores Palace’s fascination with roman statues once more, there’s a layering staple for everyone this upcoming season.

Palace’s Holiday 2022 collection can be seen in the lookbook above. The first drop of the season is set to arrive on November 25, landing in-store and online in the U.K. at 11 a.m. GMT, online in the EU at 12 p.m. CET, online in the U.S. at 11 a.m. EST/8 a.m. PST, and in-store in New York and L.A. at their respective 11 a.m. timezones. It will come to Japan on November 26 in-store and online at 11 a.m. JST, as well as via WeChat at 11 a.m. CST.
