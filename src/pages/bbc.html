Billionaire Boys Club EU Offers Up Retro Football-Inspired Kits

Kits are available in shades of black, red and blue.

With the World Cup 2022 underway, it’s the perfect moment to share one’s pride in the international sport of football. Billionaire Boys Club EU is one of many joining in the fun with the launch of a football-inspired jersey kit capsule.

The new offering centers on retro football jersey designs in three different iterations. Featuring New York City, London and Tokyo, the kits come in shades of black, red/pink and blue/white. But what brings the colors to life are their eye-catching geometric designs. Finishing off the mesh jerseys are v-neck and collared necklines and embroidered chest patches.

Retailing for £85 GBP (about $100 USD), the jersey kit capsule is available now on BBC EU’s website . Additionally, the capsule is set to launch at BBC’s London flagship store on Thursday, November 24.
