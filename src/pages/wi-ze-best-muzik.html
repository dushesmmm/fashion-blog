DJ Khaled Is Inviting You to Spend the Night in His Iconic Sneaker Closet

The record producer’s Miami haven is being listed on Airbnb.

DJ Khaled is mostly known for being a master in the studio and cooking up Billboard chart anthems with some of the biggest names in hip hop. But away from the music, he’s also recognized as one of the most prominent sneaker collectors in the game as he’s often shown off his impressive sneaker closet in one of his Miami homes. And to share a bit of his footwear haven with the fans, he’s now opening up his abode and giving folks a chance to stay in this iconic space through Airbnb.

The closet/bedroom is luxuriously designed with hardwood floors and floor-to-ceiling red shelves. The latter is filled to the brim with everything from new Jordan retro colorways to extremely rare player exclusives to grail-type installments that would have sneaker die-hards salivating at the mouth the moment they step foot inside the premises. The bed lies just beneath two framed jerseys that are styled in Bulls-inspired fashion and nod to his latest album God Did. Outside of the room sits a stunning lounge area that features a gilded couch with surrounding floral arrangements as well as a pool so that you can truly kick back and immerse yourself in the mogul’s South Beach lifestyle.

Booking for the room opens at 1pm EDT on Tuesday, November 29 for two overnight stays on December 5 and 6. During the stay, guests will be treated to an exclusive pair of his Air Jordan 5 “We the Best” collaboration, a handwritten note and a catered dinner from his restaurant The Licking.
