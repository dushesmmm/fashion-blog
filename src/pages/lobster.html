Concepts Presents 130-Piece Surreality Collection

Expanding on its upcoming Nike SB Dunk collaboration.

For well over a decade, Concepts and Nike SB have cemented themselves as champions of collaboration with their legendary “Lobster” Dunks. As they continue to write history with the introduction of 2022’s “Orange Lobster,” Concepts has prepared an immense 130-piece collection titled Surreality. When discussing the design for the duo’s SB Dunk collaboration this year, Concepts revealed it drew from surrealist art, especially the works of iconic Spanish artist Salvador Dalí who’s lobster-themed works have captivated audiences around the world.

Expanding on its surrealist inspiration, an apparel and accessories collection, partially in collaboration with the Dalí Estate, has been prepared. Pieces featuring Dalí’s art include a coach jacket, hoodie, t-shirt and bucket hat. As for the remainder of the apparel range, jackets, turtlenecks, sweaters, shirts, trousers and more provide ample opportunity to embrace Concepts’ triumphant year. Wrapping up with accessories, an “Orange Lobster” BE@RBRICK in 400% and 100% sizes has been revealed alongside others such as totes and sunglasses to complete this massive release.

Release for Concepts’ Surreality apparel and accessories collection is scheduled to take place December 1 via Concepts, the day before the official launch of Concepts’ “Orange Lobster” SB Dunk Low. Take a first look above and for the full lookbook, visit Concepts’ site.
